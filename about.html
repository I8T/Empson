Empson (U.S.A.), Inc. A Premier Wine Importer

Empson (U.S.A.), Inc. is a premium wine importer located in the Washington, D.C. metro area with warehouses in both New Jersey and California. We can help you source wine from some of the best estates in Italy, New Zealand & Chile from some of the worlds best producers of fine wines. Through its network of the country's pre-eminent wine distributors, We can deliver to any corner of the US market within days.

Over 30 years of Experience Unparalleled Portfolio.

For more than 30 years the Empsons, have set the Italian industry on the road to international fame and are still at the forefront of developments, adding exciting new producers to an unparalleled portfolio as they are uncovered. Neil is an Honorary Member of the League of Chianti and a Knight of the Order of Truffles and Wines of Alba. He travels the globe spreading the message and unearthing new treasures. Maria, artist turned business woman, oversees packaging, marketing and promotions. A number of Empson wines feature labels of her design.

Empson's personal guarantee Quality and Authenticity.

To cater to an ever increasing demand for quality wines in America, Empson (U.S.A.), Inc. was formed in 1992. It is located in the Washington, D.C. Metro Area, with warehouses in both New Jersey and California. Empson (U.S.A.) through its network of the country's pre-eminent distributors can deliver to any corner of the market within days. The Empson aim has been and always will be, to provide first-class service, supreme quality of product, with emphasis on maximizing the product/value ratio and continuity of supply to clients worldwide. The estates that are selected in this portfolio carry the Empson's personal guarantee of quality and authentisity.
